Burger Recipe

Description

BEST burger in the world.

Burger Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • Hamburger buns
  • Ketchup
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • 1 pound ground beef

Steps

  1. Season burger and apply to grill
  2. Apply cheese once burger is almost finished cooking and reduce heat
  3. Apply buns to stove and additional ingredients like onions
  4. Apply Mustard
  5. Assemble burger
